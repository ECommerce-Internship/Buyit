# Cart & Coupons

Managing your shopping cart and applying discounts.

## Your cart

Each signed-in customer has one cart. You can add a product to the cart, change the quantity of an
item, remove a single item, or clear the whole cart. The cart shows each item, its quantity and price,
and the overall total. Your cart is tied to your account, so it follows you between visits and devices.

## Stock and quantities

You can only add or raise the quantity of an item up to what the store has in stock. If an item is out
of stock you cannot add it. The cart total updates automatically as you change quantities.

## Applying a coupon

If you have a coupon code, you can apply it to your cart to get a discount. Buyit checks that the code
is valid and still active before applying it, then shows the discount and the new total. You can remove
an applied coupon to go back to the original total. Only one coupon applies to a cart at a time.

## From cart to order

When you are ready, you check out from your cart. The items, quantities, and any applied coupon carry
over into the order. See the Orders & Checkout documentation for what happens next.
