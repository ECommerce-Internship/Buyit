# Sellers & Stores

Selling on the Buyit marketplace.

## Becoming a seller

Sellers register through a dedicated seller sign-up that creates both a Seller account and their store.
A new store is reviewed and must be approved before its products become publicly visible in the catalogue.
Each seller manages their own store and the products in it.

## Managing products and inventory

Sellers add and edit their own products, set prices, upload images, and manage stock levels for each
product. When stock runs low, Buyit can raise low-stock alerts so the seller knows to restock. A seller
can only see and change products in their own store.

## AI product content

To help write listings, Buyit can generate product content with AI (Google Gemini). A seller can ask it
to draft a product description or related marketing copy, then edit and save it. This speeds up creating
good listings without writing everything from scratch.

## Bulk import

Sellers and admins can import products in bulk, including from a spreadsheet or from an SFTP file drop.
This is useful for adding or updating many products at once instead of one at a time.

## Seller dashboard

Sellers have a dashboard scoped to their own store showing sales, revenue, best-selling products, and
low-stock items. A seller only ever sees their own store's figures, never another store's or the whole
platform's.
