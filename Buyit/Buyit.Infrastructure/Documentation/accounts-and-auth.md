# Accounts & Sign-in

Buyit accounts, sign-in, and password management.

## Creating an account

You can register with an email address and a password, or sign in with Google. A standard
registration creates a **Customer** account. Sellers register through a separate seller
sign-up that also creates their store. Every account has one role: Customer, Seller, or Admin.

## Signing in with Google

Buyit supports "Sign in with Google". The first time you use it, Buyit links your Google
identity to a Buyit account and signs you in. On later visits, choosing Google signs you
straight in. You can use Google sign-in even if you first registered with a password, as long
as the email addresses match.

## Staying signed in

After you sign in, Buyit keeps you signed in using a secure token. Sessions are refreshed
automatically in the background, so you normally do not have to log in again on every visit.
Signing out ends the session on that device.

## Forgot or changing your password

If you forget your password, use "Forgot password" on the sign-in screen. Buyit emails you a
reset link that is valid for a limited time; opening it lets you set a new password. If you are
already signed in, you can change your password from your profile by entering your current
password and a new one. Google-only accounts do not have a Buyit password to reset.

## Your profile

From your profile you can update your name, phone number, and other personal details. Your email
address is tied to your sign-in and identifies your account across orders, cart, and reviews.
