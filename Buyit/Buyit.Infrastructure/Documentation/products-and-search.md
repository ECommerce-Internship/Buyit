# Products & Search

Finding products in the Buyit catalogue.

## Browsing and filtering

The catalogue can be browsed as a paged list. You can filter products by name, by category, and
by a minimum and maximum price, and sort the results. Product name matching is case-insensitive and
word-order independent, so "red running shoes" and "shoes running red" find the same items.

## Semantic search

Buyit also offers meaning-based (semantic) search. Instead of matching exact words, it matches the
*meaning* of what you type, so a search like "something to keep my coffee hot" can surface a thermos
or travel mug even if those exact words are not in the product name. Semantic search ranks products
by how close their meaning is to your query and only returns genuinely relevant results.

## Product details and stock

Each product has a name, description, price, image, category, and the store that sells it. A product
shows how many units are in stock. A product is **in stock** when its available quantity is greater
than zero; when it reaches zero it is out of stock and cannot be added to the cart.

## Reviews and ratings

Products can be reviewed by customers who have an account. A review has a star rating from 1 to 5 and
optional text. A product shows its average rating and how many reviews it has. Each customer can leave
one review per product.
