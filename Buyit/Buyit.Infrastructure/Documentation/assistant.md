# Shopping Assistant

Buyit's built-in AI chat assistant.

## What the assistant can do

Buyit has a conversational shopping assistant powered by Google Gemini. You can chat with it in plain
language to search the catalogue, ask whether something is in stock, add items to your cart, apply or
remove a coupon, and check out — all from the conversation. It remembers the context of your chat, so
you can say "yes" to a suggestion or refer back to something mentioned earlier.

## Answering questions about how Buyit works

The assistant can also answer questions about Buyit itself — how features like checkout, coupons,
returns, or seller sign-up work. For these it retrieves the relevant feature documentation and answers
based on it, rather than guessing, so the answers stay accurate and grounded in how Buyit actually works.

## What it will and won't do

The assistant always confirms with you before doing something significant such as checking out, clearing
your cart, or removing an item. It works within your permissions: a shopper gets shopping actions, and a
seller additionally gets read-only insight into their own store. It never exposes another customer's or
another store's private data, and it will not invent prices, coupon codes, or order details.
